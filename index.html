Keeping Your DeLaval Clusters in Top Condition

To maintain optimal performance and longevity of DeLaval clusters, regular maintenance is essential. This includes daily cleaning and sanitization to prevent bacterial buildup and ensure hygienic milking conditions.Changing the liners on milking machines at the recommended intervals of either 6 months or after 2500 milkings provides numerous benefits that enhance both the efficiency of the milking process and the health of the herd. Here are some key benefits:
  • Ensures optimal milk quality
  • Maximizes milking efficiency
  • Prolongs equipment lifespan
  • Reduces the risk of mastitis
  • Enhances cow comfort
  • Maintains consistent performance
  • Minimizes downtime
  • Improves hygiene and cleanliness
